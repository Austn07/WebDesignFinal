yeah, I made BREAKOUT

*Only Works on PC Right Now*
Use the arrow keys or mouse to move the paddle left and right to keep the ball from hitting the bottom! Release the ball with spacebar. (Make sure you click in the general game area before you hit the spacebar so that the page doesn't scroll.) Can you win with only three lives?

Breakout is an arcade video game developed and published by Atari, Inc. and released on May 13, 1976. It was designed by Steve Wozniak, based on conceptualization from Nolan Bushnell and Steve Bristow who were influenced by the seminal 1972 Atari arcade game Pong.
